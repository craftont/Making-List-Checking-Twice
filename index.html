Blossoms of Indiana

As the snow melts away, Indiana greets the warmth of spring with a symphony of colors. Each bloom contributes to the canvas that Mother Nature paints anew each year.

  1. Tulip Tree: Indiana's state tree blossoms with fetching yellow-green flowers.
  2. Peony: With luscious blooms, the state flower of Indiana showcases shades ranging from white to deep crimson.
  3. Wild Blue Phlox: Carpeting the woodland floor, these vibrant blue flowers signal spring's true arrival.
